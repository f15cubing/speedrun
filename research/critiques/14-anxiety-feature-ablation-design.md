## What is genuinely useful (carry forward)
- **The decision [strong]: COMPLEMENT, do not replace interleaving, and do NOT consume the single ablation slot.** Rationale is airtight: performance g ≈ 0.28 (Huntley, outliers removed) sits *right at* the detectable threshold (dz ≈ 0.3 needs ~90 learners), so an anxiety ablation at tiny-n is doomed to an uninterpretable null; running two ablated features splits power and yields two nulls. Interleaving keeps the slot.
- **The reframe that makes the feature defensible [strong]:** justify the **exam-pressure simulator on the speededness construct (SQ8) + transfer-appropriate processing**, *not* on the fragile anxiety literature. The simulator doubles as content practice, so it carries near-zero replication risk and is in-construct for the GRE. This is the single best product idea in the thread.
- **Ship cheap components as un-ablated defaults** [strong]: reappraisal microcopy (~15s card), optional skippable worry-dump, optional paced breathing — near-zero cost/risk, no performance over-claim.
- **Measurement plan [strong]:** objective **timed, no-pause, delayed (≥1 wk) held-out** score as primary (reuses the leakage-isolated bank from SQ11 — reserve a partition for this endpoint); STAI-state *after* performance as secondary; CTAS at baseline as the stratifier. The **anti-demand safeguards** (self-report secondary, neutral cover story, self-report-only win = null on primary) are exactly right given the asymmetry SQ12 documented.
- **Ablation protocol [strong]:** within-subject crossover on alternating partitions; **TOST equivalence (±2 raw items SESOI)** + honest-null template; high-CTAS top-tertile as the pre-specified subgroup; explicit power statement that this is an *estimation/feasibility* study, not a significance test.
- **Falsifiable flip condition [strong]:** elevate to co-primary only if the high-CTAS subgroup shows a >2-item benefit whose 90% CI excludes zero in a powered follow-up. Otherwise deprioritized.
- **Risk table [strong]:** names iatrogenic anxiety (worry-dump can amplify rumination for low-anxious), placebo/demand, opportunity cost, moderation, tiny-n false positives — each with a mitigation.
